What We Do

Fire Detection & Alarm Systems

We do supplies, installs and commissions ZETA fire alarm systems from GLT Exports, UK. This includes a wide product range from conventional panels, single to multiple loop addressable systems.

Fire Fighting Equipment

Supply, installation and service FFE to a wide range of clientele which includes banks, supermarket chains, oil companies, manufacturing companies, hotels, hospitals etc. The equipment includes portable fire extinguishers, hose reels, hydrants, sprinklers and fire blankets etc.

Fire Suppression Systems

We also supply and install and maintain HFC 227ea FM200 and Inergen fire suppression systems and have installed these systems in various data centres and IT rooms for banks, NGOs, manufacturing companies to name a few

Generator Sales, Installation, Repairs

Supply, installation and service all kinds of generators in buildings and institutions. This ranges from closed to open, Diesel or Petrol.

Air Conditioning Systems

We do supplies, installs and commissions air conditioning systems in offices, homes, cold rooms like server rooms

Fire Hydrants & Sprinkler systems

We also supply, install and maintain hydrants and sprinklers equipment.
